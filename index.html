Employee Assistance Program (EAP)

Shadforth provide our employees with full access to external confidential 24/7 support services. The EAP supports employees with an aim of providing timely counselling to help resolve both workplace and personal issues before they adversely impact employee wellbeing.

Employee Assistance Program (EAP)

Employees who are struggling personally or professionally, or just need someone eternal to talk to, are encouraged to make contact with the EAP services. Professional counsellors will be able to provide advice and tools to support you in your area of need, which may include (but not limited to);

  • Workplace conflict
  • Personal stress, anxiety, and depression
  • Personal trauma
  • Alcohol and or substance abuse
  • Gambling or other addictions
  • Domestic and Family violence

Contact our dedicated EAP hotline on 0407 086 000, any time you need. Should you not get straight through to the counsellor, please leave a message and they will get back to you.

Employee Referral Program

At Shadforth, we are a family company built on relationships and community. Our greatest assets are our people and often, our great people are here because someone from our team introduced them. Therefore, we are excited to announce the commencement of our Employee Referral Program.

Employee Referral Program
$5K BONUS, if your referral turns out to be one of our great assets
  • BONUS - $2,000 (payable after completion of 1 month employment)

plus

  • 12-MONTH ANNIVERSARY BONUS $3,000 (payable upon completion of 12 months employment)

*** all figures are inclusive of Superannuation and Gross, therefore taxable as normal

Focus Roles
  • Engineers
  • Foreman
  • Leading Hands
  • Skilled Operators - Final Trim and GPS Grader, Excavator, Dozer
  • Other Campaigned Roles as advertised by HR from time to time
How to Refer
  • Complete Referral on ShadConnect with your referred candidates' details
    • Name and contact details (phone and email)
    • advise what roles they are suited to,
    • what region they live in and
    • potential availability to commence - ie available now, relocation required, required to give notice with current employer)
    • How you know the candidate
    • Why you think they will fit with “The Shadforth Way”
  • Ensure you advise the person you refer, that they will receive an email from HR to confirm the employment referral and to undertake initial employment pre-screening
Terms and Conditions
  • The referrer must know the candidate personally and be able to vouch for them based on knowledge of their work or character
  • Employment periods shall always be based on first working day as recorded in Viewpoint and having worked industry expected hours ie 6.30am - 5pm Monday to Friday and Saturdays as required
  • For this Program, Employment periods may be extended or void, where periods of leave are taken.
  • To ensure transparency and ethical conduct - The referrer must not be able to influence the outcome of employment. Therefore, where a referrer is a supervisor or manager, the referred candidate must be recruited by another party and must not work on the referrers site or under the direct supervision or management of the referrer.
  • Where two or more people refer the same candidate, the first notification received by HR shall be the only person eligible for the referral bonus.
  • Only referrals submitted through ShadConnect, with a current resume shall be eligible
  • Employers are not permitted to advertise / solicit candidates on behalf of Shadforth or for this program, candidates must be known to the referrer, who in turn must be able to vouch for the candidate’s work ethics and/or character.
  • Referrers are only eligible for bonus payments whilst employed with the company. Should a referrer cease employment prior to payments falling due, the payments will no longer be payable.

Shadactive Program

The ShadActive program is designed to encourage Shadforth employees to become involved in a funded team leisure activity outside of general work hours. Team leisure activity is a collective term for various types of activities used to enhance social relations and define roles within teams, often involving collaborative tasks. Activities may include but are not limited to:

  • Sporting teams (I.e., Touch football, netball, and cricket)
  • Team building activities or camps (I.e., High ropes events, Spartan races)
  • Fitness groups (I.e., beach training, boxercise, yoga) The opportunity to become involved within a team can have a significant impact on one’s lifestyle, with the ability to improve:
    • Health and Fitness
    • Workplace Relationships
    • Morale
    • Communication Skills
    • Leadership Skills, and
    • Mateship

Shadforth will endeavour to contribute to a number of initiatives and activities throughout different stages of the year. If an activity meets a standard criterion, a panel will distribute funds accordingly to the nominated organisation.
